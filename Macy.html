Macy’s Thanksgiving Day Parade 2023: Highlights From the Holiday Event


Happy Thanksgiving, everyone! The 2023 Macy’s Thanksgiving Day Parade helped kick off the holiday season on November 23. See the best photos from the legendary parade.

It’s the most wonderful time of the year! The 2023 Macy’s Thanksgiving Day Parade aired on November 23 and featured epic performances from your favorite stars. This year, stars like Jabari Banks, Jon Batiste, En Vogue, ENHYPEN, Jimmy Fallon, and more were among the performers. Lacey Chabert, Andy Cohen, Leslie Odom Jr., Abigail Spencer, Kenan Thompson, Kel Mitchell, and more also made special appearances during the parade.

The 97th edition of the parade included iconic character balloons, amazing floats, marching bands, and so much more. Of course, the parade featured an appearance from the one and only Santa Claus. Scroll down for all the highlights!

Al Roker

Al Roker made his triumphant return to the Macy’s Thanksgiving Day Parade in 2023. He co-hosted the parade with Hoda Kotb and Savannah Guthrie.

2023 Macy’s Thanksgiving Day Parade

It’s officially the holiday season! New York City celebrated Thanksgiving with the 2023 Macy’s Thanksgiving Day Parade on November 23.

Snoopy Float

It’s not Thanksgiving without an inflatable Snoopy! The massive Snoopy float made its way through New York City during the 2023 Macy’s Thanksgiving Day Parade.

Jimmy Fallon

Jimmy Fallon was one of the celebrities on hand at the 2023 Macy’s Thanksgiving Day Parade. He bundled up in a black coat and a navy scarf.

Miss America Grace Stanke

Miss America Grace Stanke looked stunning at the 2023 Macy’s Thanksgiving Day Parade. She wowed in a red sequin dress and red fur coat.

Brandy

Brandy waved to New Yorkers during the Macy’s Thanksgiving Day Parade. She rode on Brach’s Palace of Sweets float.

Bell Biv DeVoe

Bell Biv DeVoe rode the Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles: Mutant Mayhem float during the 2023 Macy’s Thanksgiving Day Parade. The group also performed during the parade.

En Vogue

The ladies of En Vogue looked radiant during the 2023 Macy’s Thanksgiving Day Parade. The group performed atop the Colossal Wave of Wonder float.

Jon Batiste

Jon Batiste performed during the 2023 Macy’s Thanksgiving Day Parade. He helped kick off the holiday event!

Ashley Park

Ashley Park got into the holiday spirit at the 2023 Macy’s Thanksgiving Day Parade. She looked cozy in a plaid coat and matching headband.

ENHYPEN

ENHYPEN performed during the 2023 Macy’s Thanksgiving Day Parade. The group rode on the Baby Shark float during the parade.

Kung Fu Panda Float

Hi-ya! A Kung Fu Panda float was a part of the Macy’s Thanksgiving Day Parade line-up this year.

Kenan Thompson and Kel Mitchell

Kenan Thompson and Kel Mitchell celebrated Thanksgiving by attending the Macy’s Thanksgiving Day Parade together. The co-stars have teamed up onscreen again for Good Burger 2.

Paul Russell

Paul Russell waved to paradegoers during the 2023 Macy’s Thanksgiving Day Parade. The singer made his parade debut this year.
